Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report extremism anonymously?

Use our secure online reporting forms or call/text our Signal hotline. You are not required to provide personal information, and all submissions are encrypted by Signal.

What happens after I submit a report?

Our intelligence team reviews all reports. Verified threats are immediately shared with relevant authorities or affiliated social media accounts for public awareness, depending on the threat's nature.

How do you protect my privacy?

We use military-grade encryption for all communications and data storage. Personal information is never shared without explicit consent, and anonymous reporting is fully supported.

Can I volunteer or partner with Terror Alarm?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with organizations, governments, and individuals committed to combating extremism. Contact us at support@terroralarm.org to discuss opportunities.
